Our Story

We began with a simple idea: coffee can be powerful when it honors both taste and people. Founded by a group of travelers and roasters, Aurora Coffee partners directly with producers to bring forward coffees that carry place, craft and care.

Why direct-trade matters

Direct trade allows us to pay above-market prices, invest in community projects, and build long-term relationships. It means knowing who grew your coffee and why the flavors exist.

Our Roasting Philosophy

We roast to highlight the coffee’s origin — not to hide it. Profiles are developed to bring forward clarity, sweetness, and nuance. Our roasters taste with intention and roast with restraint.
